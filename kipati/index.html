Why Design Kipati?

Financial institutions worldwide lose a whooping $3.6 billion to penalties for non-compliance

Kipati was an effort to unravel the source of this failure, and leverage technology to prevent non-compliance.

Non-compliance can hurt a business' customers, reputation and profits. The biggest source of this failure is a breach in communication and a lack of accountability.

Key Insights from UX Research

Cloth donors need more options and information

100% Cared

They agreed that cloths should be accessible to everyone

80% were Unaware

They mostly didn't know where to donate outside churchs and mosques

60% would Volunteer

They would love to go out to do the donation in-person

50% were Relucant

They were hesitant to donate cloths for superstitious and cultural reasons
