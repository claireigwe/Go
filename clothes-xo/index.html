Donate with absolute ease to clothe others on ClothesXO

Donors no longer need to haul clothes to far-away donation points to get donations to people in need. ClothesXO takes care of the big and small issues in cloth donations

Product Overview

Design Challenge

There is joy in giving. However, only few people can afford the time and energy to find places to make donations and get in touch with folks in need directly. Worse, in cultures where cloths carry spiritual significance and there is stigma around collecting donate cloths, donating cloths become a sensitive issue

Design Solution

ClothesXO is a mobile app designed to help users find cloth donation points, schedule pick ups, join or donate money to cloth donation campaigns instead.

The Challenge

Why Clothes XO?

Donating cloths physically can be too much work

Folks won't donate clothes because:

  • They can't find time to go out donating
  • They don't know any donor campaigns
  • They can't find drop-off points
  • They can't get cloths to drop-off points
  • They hold superstitions around cloths

The Solution

Find and take part in cloth donation campaigns from one app, Clothes XO

Donating cloths can be as rewarding as it is convenient

With Clothes XO, anyone can find and take part in cloth donation outreaches from the convenience of an app. The MVP helps users:

  • Find drop-off points for their donations
  • Find and volunteers for outreaches
  • Book a pick up for cloth donations
  • Donate money instead of cloths

Design Thinking

The love and thoughts behind Clothes XO, in brief

Empathize

I prepared and conducted a short survey and interviews with five (5) willing participants. Their responses were evaluated on an empathy map

Ideate

After brainstorming with a dear friend, the idea was to design a produc that took out any inconvenience and worry around donating cloths

Prototype

We created a high-fidelity prototype for a mobile app designed to help users find and join cloth donation campaigns with clothes, in cash or in kind.

Test

Successful usability tests by potential users and a mentor at the Zuri HNG program revealed the need for improvements to the UX Copy and the navigation

The Process: UX Research

The Product Designer's Process from Drawing Insights

UX Research Method

The plan was to draw qualitative insights from semi-structured user interviews and quantitative data from a users surveys. The sample size taken and the scope of this study were limited by the project's (2-Week) timeframe

Research Goal

  • How do users typically make donations, especially cloths?
  • What challenges do users experience while trying to donate cloths?
  • Would a cloth donation mobile app be a relevant product?

UX Research Findings

What Users research revealed

100%

All the participants agree that cloths should be accessible to everyone. Clothes XO is a valuable solution

80%

Four-fifths of the participants did not know anywhere else they could donate outside religious centers

60%

Nearly two-thirds of the participants wanted to be physical parts of cloth donation campaigns

50%

Half of them were reluctant about donating their cloths. They feared it might be used in voodoo
