Why Design immers?

Online fashion shoppers always worry that cloths they order won't fit

Size and color labels can be both confusing and misleading. And both shoppers and online retailers hate it when cloths get sent back. The alternatives are too expensive.

Fixing the confusion of clothing sizing for cloth shoppers would make fashion shopping a lot more inclusive and enjoyable, for everyone

Big Insigts from UX Research

Fashion shoppers just want to be sure that what they order is what they get

90% Got poor fits

At least once, they had gotten cool cloths that didn't fit right

72% Misled by Labels

Shoppers felt they had been misled by size and color labels in listings

67% Misled by Mannequins

Shoppers felt misled by mannequins with uncommonly perfect figures

50% Can't go Shopping

They still found shopping online to be the more convenient option
