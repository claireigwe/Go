Shop and Try out cloths from anywhere on an easy-to-use virtual reality store, Immers

With Immers, what you order is always what you get. Fashion shoppers can virtually try out cloths from their favorite stores, anywhere and anytime

Product Overview

Design Challenge

Online fashion shoppers always worry whether the cloths they order from online stores would actually fit them. It was always safer to just walk into a physical store to try out cloths. But this meant more stress for fashion shoppers and a real problem for online fashion retailers.

Design Solution and Process

We combined insights from fashion shoppers and vendors to build Immers - an easy-to-use virtual fashion store with life-sized 3d model/mannequin of shoppers who wanted to try out cloths from their favorite stores online

The Challenge

Why Immers?

50% of the US shoppers return cloths to online stores because the sizes don't fit

Size and color labels was both confusing and misleading. And both shoppers and online retailers hate it when cloths get sent back. The alternatives were too costly and inconvenient

  • Send cloths to shopper for test
  • Open physical stores near every shopper

The Solution

Virtual Reality Fashion Store for Trying Out Cloths Online

Design for Dashboard

Beyond the financial penalties, businesses also risk criminal prosecution and the irredeemable loss of reputation from missed compliance deadlines

Design Thinking

he sytematic process behind the thoughts that went into Immers

Empathize

Our research noted the struggles of retailers with online shoppers returning cloths that don't fit. Trying cloths physicaly had become inconvenient

Ideate

Our brainstorming sessions proposed a VR mobile app that allowed shoppers to try out cloths remotely using mannequins modeled from a full-body scan

Prototype

We created high-fidelity wireframes for a mobile VR store app with screens for body scan, finding cloths, trying out cloths, check-outs, and Sign Up/Sign In

Test

With a high-fidelity prototype, we evaluated our product's effectiveness and ease of use with respect to design heuristic principles

The UI/UX design for Immers used insights from open-ended interview and surveys involving involving young online fashion shoppers. It also included a competitor analysis of online fashion retail applicatons
