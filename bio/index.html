Hi there! I am Claire Chisom Igwe.

My friends think my job is creating beautiful prototypes on Figma. My students would say I can't stop obsessing over responsive design using Figma auto-layouts, reusable UI components, and better design hand-offs. My clients trust me to find a quick, human-centered and elegant way to build the backbone for digital products that draw in their most delighted customers.They are all correct...

For nearly 3 years, I have worked with businesses in diverse industries to accelerate their journeys towards shipping successful digital products. As a User Interface and User Experience (UI/UX) designer with a keen interest in interaction design, I drive success to products by:

UX Research

Explore user behavior and pains to anticipate and improve their interaction with digital products

Design Systems

Manage and update a comprehensive library of UI design assets to accelerate design and streamline development

Figma Prototype

Design interactive prototypes that are beautiful, easy-to-code and functional

UX Audits

Implement UX audits and test prototypes to evaluate usabilty

No-Code Development

Turn Web Designs into live websites with Webflow, HTML and CSS

But I wasn't born a Product designer. I'd love to meet anyone who was. My journey to UI/UX design is one love story I love to tell, and I can bet you would love to hear it.

From writing blissful kid stories to designing user interfaces that make for an unforgetable experience

I always loved a good story: rom-com, crime thriller, feminist, you name it! But great stories are hard to come by. So, I chose to write them. But I always wanted to do more than tell stories. I wanted to build memories with every single story.

When I stumbled upon UI/UX design, I was a creative storywriter. I loved to write stories that fill the mind and hearts of kids with beautiful colors and life lessons. I also deeply enjoyed telling stories around real and pressing gender issues. So, I created a gender and culture blog "NotJustTalks."

This blog was my way of journaling the joys and pains of women in my community. It was all about representation, equalitity, inclusion, gender, and social justice. It had always been a passion to share my story and the stories of the everyday women that inspire me everyday.

Managing this blog on Wordpress introduced me to the fascinating world of content design, web development using CMS, digital marketing, and product management. At first, I just wanted to improve the look and feel of my blog beyond the Wordpress templates. So, front-end development with HTML, CSS, and Javascript instantly caught my fancy

But then, I naturally stumbled upon UI/UX design. And I literally got hooked for life. Finally, I found a platform that allowed me do more than tell unforgetable stories. I could actually build experiences that digital product user find so delightful that they are unforgetable.

My big dreams...
