Hi! Claire here! I'm a Digital Product Designer

But, what does this mean?

My friends think that my job is creating beautiful prototypes on Figma. They think I can't stop obsessing over auto-layouts, critiquing products, and pushing for better design hand-off. My clients trust me to find a reproducible way to build the backbone for digiatl products that drive successful businesses and their most delighted customers.

I know they are both correct...

Went from Kid Stories to User Experience Design...

How it all began...

When I fell madly in love with digital product design, I was proactively exploring opportunities to build value for businesses while creating a happier world for people. More than two years ago, I managed a blog that chronicled the struggles of women in my local community. I wrote short stories for kids and brands on freelance gigs, and got my hands dirty with everything from digital marketing to front-end development.

Every step in this journey was a colorful opportunity to learn something new about people, products, and passion...My colorful background ensures that I care deeply about diverse aspects of the product design and development process, whether it is advocating for better UX copies and design hand-offs.

Working as a Product Designer has been exciting...

See some of the work I've done...

After more than two years designing products, my biggest superpower has become helping businesses build beautiful UI prototypes that form the backbone for digital products users love. My clients trust my mastery of design tools like Figma. It has been a thrill to bring products from concept to development using best-practices in responsive design with autolayouts, managing UI libraries on complex projects, and documentation.

In the past year, I've worked as a product designer on diverse teams, building everything from a digital registry for vetted eco-friendly consumer products to a blockchain-powered real estate SaaS.

It has also been a privilege to serve alongside my mentors at HerTechTrail as a product design (UI/UX) coach, empowering hundreds of women starting out as product designers with the knowledge and support they need to excel.

This is a joyful full circle journey back to a community that kickstarted my product design story. Product design has given me a platform to help women build the skills they need to create a happier world
